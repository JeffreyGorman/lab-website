PhD Studentships

Externally-funded PhD positions will be advertised online when available. There are also other funding sources we can explore together – contact us to discuss options including:

  • SCOPE/EPSRC DLA
  • Chinese Scholarship Council Scholarships

Postdoctoral Fellowships

Grant-funded postdoctoral fellowships will be advertised online when available. I’m happy to support applicants for external individual fellowships – please get in touch with a CV and cover letter as soon as possible.

Fellowship opportunities include:

  • Royal Commission for the Exhibition of 1851 Fellowship – for early career STEM researchers
  • Leverhulme Trust Early Career Fellowship – for researchers without permanent posts
  • Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Fellowship – for researchers moving within Europe
  • Newton International Fellowship – for non-UK scientists wishing to work in the UK
  • EMBO Postdoctoral Fellowship – to support international mobility
  • Career Development Fellowship – for researchers from Black heritage backgrounds
  • Daphne Jackson Fellowship – for those returning after a career break

Undergraduate Summer Internships

Limited paid undergraduate research opportunities are available. Please get in touch well ahead of deadlines to draft the strongest application possible.
