## Postdoctoral Fellowships

Grant-funded postdoctoral fellowships will be advertised online when available. I'm happy to support applicants for external individual fellowships – please [get in touch](mailto:jeffrey.e.gorman@durham.ac.uk) with a CV and cover letter as soon as possible.

**Fellowship opportunities include:**

- [Royal Commission for the Exhibition of 1851 Fellowship](https://royalcommission1851.org/) – for early career STEM researchers
- [Leverhulme Trust Early Career Fellowship](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/early-career-fellowships) – for researchers without permanent posts
- [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions Fellowship](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships) – for researchers moving within Europe
- [Newton International Fellowship](https://royalsociety.org/grants/newton-international/) – for non-UK scientists wishing to work in the UK
- [EMBO Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.embo.org/funding/fellowships-grants-and-career-support/postdoctoral-fellowships/) – to support international mobility
- [Career Development Fellowship](https://royalsociety.org/grants/career-development-fellowship/) – for researchers from Black heritage backgrounds
- [Daphne Jackson Fellowship](https://daphnejackson.org/) – for those returning after a career break
