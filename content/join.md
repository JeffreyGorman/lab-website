We welcome applicants with backgrounds in:

- synthetic organic chemistry
- biosciences
- materials chemistry/spectroscopy

We are especially keen to train cross-disciplinary scientists. If you're interested, please [get in touch](mailto:jeffrey.e.gorman@durham.ac.uk).

## PhD studentships

Externally-funded PhD positions will be advertised online. There might also exist other funding sources: [get in touch](mailto:jeffrey.e.gorman@durham.ac.uk) to discuss the available options, for example:

- [SCOPE/EPSRC DLA](https://www.durham.ac.uk/departments/academic/science/research-excellence/science-doctoral-training/pgr-funding-routes/)
- [Chinese Scholarship Council Scholarships](https://www.durham.ac.uk/study/scholarships/international/chinese-scholarships-council/) 

## Postdoctoral fellowships

Grant-funded postdoctoral fellowships will be advertised online when available. I am very happy to support applicants for external individual fellowships. Please [get in touch](mailto:jeffrey.e.gorman@durham.ac.uk) with a CV and cover letter ASAP to discuss those further. Some fellowship opportunities are listed below:

- [Royal Commission for the Exhibition of 1851 Fellowship](https://royalcommission1851.org/) 
- [Leverhulme Trust Early Career Fellowship](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/early-career-fellowships)
- [Marie Sk≈Çodowska-Curie Actions Fellowship](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships) for EU.
- [Newton International Fellowship](https://royalsociety.org/grants/newton-international/) for internationals.
- [EMBO Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.embo.org/funding/fellowships-grants-and-career-support/postdoctoral-fellowships/) for internationals.
- [Career Development Fellowship](https://royalsociety.org/grants/career-development-fellowship/) for (mixed) Black backgrounds.
- [Daphne Jackson Fellowship](https://daphnejackson.org/) for returning after a career break.
